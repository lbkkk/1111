iVolunteer Vietnam

iVolunteer Vietnam is a community project dedicated to sharing, compiling, and providing information about various activities such as volunteering, training, seminars, conferences, and youth forums on social issues both domestically and internationally for students.
The project aims to build a strong, beneficial community, enriching the lives of Vietnamese students with positive values and opportunities.

THINGS I DO

Contribution

As a Media Ambassador for iVolunteer Vietnam, I utilized my personal brand to disseminate useful and necessary information on a wide range of topics, including volunteering, free courses, and study abroad opportunities.
This role has been instrumental in enhancing my skills in information filtering, selection, and presentation. I've learned to design and tailor information to best suit the needs and interests of student audiences.

Achievement

Through this experience, I've also developed a deeper understanding of the importance of effective communication in social media and the power of digital platforms in mobilizing and educating the youth.
It has taught me the intricacies of engaging with a diverse audience, ensuring that the content is not only informative but also inspiring and motivational.
Additionally, working with iVolunteer Vietnam has honed my skills in creating compelling narratives that resonate with young people, encouraging them to take action and participate in community and global initiatives.

What I learned from this job position

Furthermore, this experience has expanded my network, allowing me to collaborate with a variety of stakeholders including NGOs, educational institutions, and fellow youth advocates.
It has been a journey of personal and professional growth, deepening my commitment to social causes and enhancing my abilities as a communicator and a leader in the community.
