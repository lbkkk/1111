THINGS I DO

Contribution

Once the collection was conceptualized, I spearheaded the development of the communication strategy. This involved identifying the most effective platforms and mediums to reach our target audience.
Knowing that Gen Z is highly active on digital platforms, I focused on social media channels, influencer collaborations, and digital marketing tactics.One of my key contributions was in content creation for our digital campaigns.
I came up with the idea of opening a cultural museum in a short time to attract Gen Z customers to learn more about culture, craft villages and the beauty of jewelry.

What I learned from this job position

Through this project, I developed several key skills. My research abilities were enhanced as I delved into understanding Gen Z's fashion preferences and digital behaviors. This also improved my trend analysis skills, enabling me to predict and align with upcoming fashion trends effectively.
In terms of strategic planning, I honed my ability to develop and implement a multi-channel communication strategy. This required not only creative ideation but also practical execution, including scheduling, managing, and analyzing the performance of our digital campaigns.
