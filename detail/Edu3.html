Contribution

A pivotal element of this project was the strategic marketing of the doctoral program. I delved deeply into the program's distinct attributes and strengths. This comprehensive understanding was instrumental in crafting a compelling value proposition, resonating with potential candidates and setting the program apart in a competitive academic landscape.
The core of my strategy involved a thorough analysis of our target demographic: individuals who are considering a doctoral journey. By understanding their aspirations, needs, and decision-making processes, I was able to tailor our marketing strategies. This approach ensured that our messaging was not only relevant but also engaging to this unique audience.
In today's digital age, where information channels are varied and vast, I recognized the importance of a multi-faceted marketing approach. Hence, I formulated a multi-channel strategy, encompassing digital media, interactive events, and collaborations with academic networks. This broad-based strategy was aimed at maximizing our outreach and resonating with potential Ph.D. students on platforms and environments where they are most active.

What I learned from this job position

This project was not just an exercise in applying theoretical knowledge but also a practical venture into the nuanced world of academic marketing. It allowed me to explore and implement diverse marketing techniques and strategies, ultimately contributing to the heightened visibility and appeal of the University of Economics Ho Chi Minh City's doctoral program.
