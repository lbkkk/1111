SPRITE x MUSIC

A project was created to participate in the CMO 2023 competition. Although the project did not win an award, the judges' feedback was quite accurate according to the given criteria.
With the goal of promoting the SPRITE product closely associated with music, we developed a communication plan to attract the younger generation to a new cultural experience, associating Sprite with music.
So, how did I and my creative team execute this plan?

THINGS I DO

Contribution

Our communication plan was designed to reach a wide audience of young people. This initiative includes a series of activities, including a reality music series, a TikTok challenge, and a music concert.
One of the challenges we faced was developing a creative concept that would resonate with young people. I wanted to create something that was both authentic and engaging. So, we ultimately decided to focus on the idea of music as a form of self-expression.
I had the idea of creating a video series in which young people from all walks of life share their personal stories through music, with the sound from Sprite.

What I learned from this job position

Through this experience, I have honed several key skills. My analytical abilities have sharpened, enabling me to effectively dissect market data and derive meaningful insights.
My creativity in content creation and marketing strategy development has also been enhanced. Additionally, I've developed strong digital marketing skills, including social media management and content scheduling.
Moreover, this project improved my teamwork and collaboration skills, as it required close coordination with various team members, from market researchers to designers and digital marketers. I learned the importance of clear communication and the value of diverse perspectives in creating a successful marketing campaign.
In summary, my involvement in this project for the Createch Marketers 2023 competition has been a significant learning journey, equipping me with a robust set of marketing skills and contributing to our team's notable achievement in the competition.
