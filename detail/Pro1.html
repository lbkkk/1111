TVPharm x Createch Marketers

This is a project created to participate in the Createch Marketers 2023 competition, and it has helped our team secure the runner-up position in the competition.
With the goal of promoting the Phariton Active product to a new target audience aged 22-25 who are just starting their careers, we have developed a communication plan to help the TV Pharm brand better understand both existing and potential customers.
We aimed to bring marketing strategies closer to this target audience.

THINGS I DO

Contribution

In this project, my role was multifaceted.
Firstly, I conducted in-depth market research to identify the specific needs and preferences of our target audience. This involved analyzing current trends, consumer behavior, and competitor strategies to ensure our approach was both innovative and relevant.
Secondly, I played a key role in developing the core messaging and content for our marketing campaigns. This required me to utilize my creative skills to craft engaging and persuasive content that resonates with young professionals. I also worked to ensure that the visual elements of our campaign aligned with our messaging and appealed to our target audience.
Furthermore, I was actively involved in the digital marketing aspect of the project. I managed social media campaigns, utilizing platforms popular among our target demographic. This involved scheduling posts, engaging with users, and analyzing social media metrics to gauge the effectiveness of our strategies and make necessary adjustments.

What I learned from this job position

Through this experience, I have honed several key skills. My analytical abilities have sharpened, enabling me to effectively dissect market data and derive meaningful insights. My creativity in content creation and marketing strategy development has also been enhanced.
Additionally, I've developed strong digital marketing skills, including social media management and content scheduling.
Moreover, this project improved my teamwork and collaboration skills, as it required close coordination with various team members, from market researchers to designers and digital marketers. I learned the importance of clear communication and the value of diverse perspectives in creating a successful marketing campaign.
In summary, my involvement in this project for the Createch Marketers 2023 competition has been a significant learning journey, equipping me with a robust set of marketing skills and contributing to our team's notable achievement in the competition.
