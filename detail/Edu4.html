Loving homes(Mái ấm tình thương) by AD001 (UEH)

Participating in the "Loving Homes (Mái ấm tình thương)" initiative organized by class AD001 at UEH was a deeply rewarding experience that allowed me to contribute meaningfully to the community while applying and expanding my skills.
As a member of the organizing team, I was involved in the end-to-end planning of the project. This included setting objectives, strategizing activities, allocating resources, and overseeing the execution of planned activities. My focus was on ensuring that our initiatives were impactful and well-coordinated.
