Xanh Yên

Xanh Yen Staycation is an exciting startup project that I had the privilege to work on alongside a dedicated team of collaborators. This venture reflects our shared vision to create a unique and refreshing experience in the realm of staycations, blending tranquility with modern amenities.
The project not only allowed us to channel our passion for creating memorable getaways but also provided a practical platform to put our marketing skills into action.

THINGS I DO

Contribution

As a Marketing Assistant, when Xanh Yen was established, I had several tasks that needed to be completed. These included setting up a Fanpage on Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok, creating videos and reels to promote services, designing graphics, and generating content to attract potential customers. Additionally, I responded to customer inquiries daily on the Meta platform.

Achievement

After almost 6 months of operation, Xanh Yen has achieved some noteworthy milestones. We have garnered approximately 6,000 followers across three platforms, ensured full bookings from the opening, and attracted over 300,000 views on social media.

What I learned from this job position

Furthermore, my scope of work expanded to include crafting informative articles for Xanh Yen Staycation, showcasing my ability to create engaging written content. This multifaceted experience has significantly honed my skills in social media management, content creation, design, and customer interaction. It has also taught me the intricacies of brand storytelling and effective communication, further enhancing my capabilities in these areas. Beyond these skills, I honed my cultural sensitivity and emotional intelligence, building genuine connections with guests from diverse social media platforms.
This journey has proven my dedication and adaptability within the marketing field, and I'm eager to leverage these newly acquired strengths in my next challenge.
