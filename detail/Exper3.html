Saigon City Residence

Saigon City Residence is a high-rise serviced apartment building located in the heart of Ho Chi Minh City. The building is a popular destination for both locals and tourists alike, especially Japanese people living and working in Vietnam.

THINGS I DO

Contribution

Working at Saigon City Residence as a Customer Officer, my role encompassed a variety of responsibilities. I collaborated closely with the marketing team to develop customer outreach strategies, ensuring that our communications effectively reached and engaged our target audience.
My daily routine involved reporting tasks and progress to management, providing them with a clear picture of our day-to-day operations and any challenges encountered. A significant part of my job was using OTA to attract potential customers. This responsibility allowed me to understand customer behavior and preferences deeply, guiding our team in tailoring our services to better meet their needs.
Additionally, communicating with international clients in English honed my language skills and cultural awareness, enabling me to effectively bridge communication gaps.
Through these tasks, I have learned the importance of adaptability and strategic thinking in customer service. I developed a keen sense of how to anticipate customer needs and respond proactively.

Achievement

Last but not least, my role has greatly improved my skills in cross-cultural communication, making me adept at handling diverse client queries and concerns. This experience has been instrumental in refining my problem-solving abilities, particularly in high-pressure situations.
By regularly interacting with a wide range of clients, I've also cultivated a patient and empathetic approach, which is crucial in any customer-facing role.

What I learned from this job position

Overall, my tenure at Saigon City Residence has been a journey of continuous learning and professional development, equipping me with the essential skills and insights needed to excel in customer service and relations in a global context.
