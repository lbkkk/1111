THINGS I DO

Contribution

In this project, I embarked on an exciting journey to develop a targeted advertising campaign for ELSA Speak, an advanced AI-powered language learning application. My mission was to craft engaging and impactful promotional materials aimed at young professionals aged 22-28, a demographic keen on integrating technology into their personal and professional development.
Through my involvement in writing content and creating simple designs for the ELSA Speak advertising campaign, I gained a wealth of insights and valuable skills.
One of the most crucial lessons was the importance of deeply understanding the target audience. Crafting messages that resonate with young professionals required me to delve into their aspirations, challenges, and preferences, ensuring that our content was not just informative but also relatable and engaging. Developing ad content honed my skills in persuasive writing.
I learned how to effectively highlight the unique selling points of ELSA Speak and present them in a way that was convincing and compelling, driving the audience towards a specific call to action.
Working on the design aspect taught me the power of simplicity. In today's fast-paced digital world, capturing the audience's attention quickly is crucial. I learned how to create visually appealing designs that are not overly complicated yet convey the message clearly and effectively.

What I learned from this job position

This experience underscored the importance of continual learning and adaptation in the field of content creation and design. Staying updated with the latest trends and being willing to adapt and experiment with new ideas were crucial for the success of the campaign. It is also a significant learning journey, enhancing my skills in content creation, design, and strategic communication, all of which I believe are essential in my ongoing career development.
